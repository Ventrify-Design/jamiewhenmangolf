The Coach

Jamie Whenman

Jamie Whenman is a PGA Master Professional with over 18 years of competitive tour experience, including 12 professional wins across the European and Asian Tours. After retiring from competitive play in 2018, Jamie channeled his deep technical knowledge and performance psychology into coaching — helping players at every level unlock their true potential.

His approach blends biomechanical precision with elite mental performance frameworks used by top-100 players worldwide. Whether you're a club-level amateur or a touring professional, Jamie delivers results that last.
