The Coach

Jamie Whenman

Jamie Whenman is a British Advanced PGA Professional whose career has taken him across five countries — the UK, USA, UAE, Indonesia, and Singapore. He turned professional at 18 and competed in European PGA events before channelling his competitive instincts into coaching, where he has built an extraordinary international reputation.

His coaching philosophy is rooted in swing mechanics — building repeatable, efficient, and personalised swings based on how each player's body naturally moves. Trained under legendary instructor David Leadbetter and certified across nine disciplines including TPI, Swing Catalyst, and PEAK, Jamie brings genuinely world-class methodology to every lesson. Whether you're a club amateur or an aspiring tour professional, the Whenman method delivers results that last.
